    Apache RocketMQ

    Apache RocketMQ® is an open source distributed messaging and streaming data platform.
    Latest source v4.0.0

    Getting Started

    Low Latency

    More than 99.6% response latency within 1 milliseconds under high pressure.

    Finance Oriented

    High availability with replica. Every message could be tracked or repaired with a serial of tracking tools.

    BigData Friendly

    Batch transferring with versatile integration for flooding throughput.

    Industry Sustainable

    Trillions of messaging capabilities support Alibaba Group’s 11.11 Global Shopping Festival.

    Massive Accumulation

    Accumulate unlimited messages as long as there is enough disk space without performance loss.

    Vendor Neutral

    A new open distributed messaging standard since latest 4.x version.
